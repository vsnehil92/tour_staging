SOUTHERN GRAND TOUR, India

Goa-Beaches, India

Experience the beauty of golden sandy beaches, lush countryside ambience and a spectacular sunset view of India's beach town, Goa. Crafted especially for women travellers, the tour packs some of the most exciting activities that you could indulge in with your gang. On the day of your arrival, you will go for a dinner at the beautiful Antares Restaurant & Beach Club. On day two, you would get to enjoy a sightseeing tour in North Goa and party the night away at one of the most happening venues in the town. Day three is going to be a sheer delight for adventure enthusiasts as it includes Jet Ski ride and surfing. Day four is packed with experiences like lunch at Copper Leaf, a relaxing yacht ride and a delicious dinner at Thalassa. Don't wait further. Pick this tour and head for an exciting holiday in Goa.
